Flammkuchen

I like Flammkuchen because it's super delicious and very traditional for my region. Whenever I have visitors from abroad, I like to cook this dish so they can experience a truly local meal.

Ingredients:

  • Dough
  • Crème fraîche
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Bacon cut into cubes

Preparation

Spread crème fraîche across the dough. Place thinley chopped onions and bacon on top and cover with cheese. Bake in the oven at 200°C for 12 minutes.
