Lentil Soup

Living in a foreign country far away from my family, has its ups and downs. As much I love travelling and exploring the world, there are always times when I miss home. And there is no better remedy for these moments than my Mama’s scrumptious lentil soup. It’s a simple dish, really. But there is something about it that fills my belly with love and makes me feel at ease.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Leek
  • 1 Onion
  • 5 Carrots
  • 3 Potatoes
  • 250 g Green lentils
  • Celery
  • Vegetable broth
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter
  • Mettwurst sausages
  • Apple Cider Vinegar

Preparation

Cut all the vegetables into little chunks. Take a spoonful of butter and heat it up in a big pot. Then steam the onion and leeks. Add the carrots, celery, and lentils. Fill the pot with water. Let it boil. Add the potatoes. Add the vegetable broth, salt, and pepper. Throw in the sausages. Let it simmer. Prickle the sausages for their juice to add some flavour. When the veg is firm to bite, serve. If you want, you can add a spoonful or two of vinegar. Voilà. Enjoy :)
